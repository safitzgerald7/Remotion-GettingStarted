## Transitions
- Easing: cubic-bezier(0.25, 0.46, 0.45, 0.94)
- Fade In Duration: 2.5 seconds
- Scale Duration: 1.5 seconds
- Float Duration: 1 second
- Sway Duration: 2 seconds
- Slide Duration: 1 second (for slide transitions)

## Animation Guidelines
- Use interpolate for smooth animations
- Extrapolate: clamp
- Easing: bezier for organic feel
- Opacity for fades
- Scale for growth
- TranslateY for floating
- Rotate for sway
- TranslateX for slides
